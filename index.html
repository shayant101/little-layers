Our story

Big ideas, one layer at a time

Little Layers began with a simple belief: the things kids use every day should be fun and genuinely useful. Every organizer, caddy and tray is designed and 3D-printed to order — bright, sturdy, and built to keep crayons, markers and treasures in their place.

Founded by Irha, age 7, in Irvine, California.
